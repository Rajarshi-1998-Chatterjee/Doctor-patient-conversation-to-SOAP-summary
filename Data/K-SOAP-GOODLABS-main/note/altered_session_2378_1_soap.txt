Diagnosis: Suspected angina.

Plan
Suspected angina:
1. EKG and possibly a stress test to evaluate heart function.
2. Prescribe nitroglycerin for chest pain episodes.
3. Start low-dose aspirin regimen for blood clot prevention.
4. Referral to a dietitian for diabetes management.
5. Influenza vaccine administration today.
6. Follow-up in two weeks to reassess symptoms and review test results.
7. Instructed to go to the emergency room immediately if chest pain becomes severe or if new symptoms like fainting occur.
