Plan
Angina Pectoris:
1. Order an ECG and consider an exercise stress test.
2. Refer to a cardiologist for further evaluation.
3. Prescribe nitroglycerin for chest pain as needed.
4. Continue atorvastatin for cholesterol management.
5. Avoid strenuous activities until further assessment.
6. Discuss heart-healthy diet and potential cardiac rehabilitation exercises in the future.
7. Schedule follow-up in two weeks to review test results and assess progress.
8. Advise to seek emergency care for severe chest pain, shortness of breath, or fainting.
9. Educate on the importance of getting the influenza vaccine.
